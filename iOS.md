## KeeWeb

We don't have an own app, but there's a workaround. However it's recommended to use a native app instead because they provide better user experience and integration with built-in security features of iOS.

1. open [app.keeweb.info](https://app.keeweb.info) in Safari
2. tap Share
3. select Add to Home Screen
